Breakfast & Starters

Tapsilog (Tapa, Sinangag, Itlog)
P99
Tapsilog is a classic Filipino breakfast dish consisting of three components: Tapa (marinated beef or sometimes pork), Sinangag (garlic fried rice), and Itlog (fried egg).
Longganisa with Garlic Rice and Fried Egg
P99
Longganisa is a Filipino sausage with a slightly sweet and garlicky flavor. It's typically served with garlic fried rice and a fried egg. Each region in the Philippines may have its own variation of longganisa, offering a diverse range of flavors.
Tocino with Garlic Rice and Fried Egg
P99
Tocino is a sweet and savory cured pork dish that's thinly sliced and usually pan-fried until caramelized. It's commonly served with garlic rice and a fried egg, creating a delightful combination of flavors.
Bangus (Milkfish) with Garlic Rice and Salted Egg
P99
Bangus, or milkfish, is a popular fish in the Philippines, and it's often served for breakfast. It is usually marinated and pan-fried to perfection. Accompanied by garlic rice and a side of salted egg, this dish offers a mix of flavors and textures that Filipinos adore.

LUNCH & DINNER

Adobo
P69
Adobo is considered the national dish of the Philippines. It involves marinating meat (commonly pork or chicken) in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices, then simmering it until tender and flavorful.
Sinigang
P69
Sinigang is a sour soup made with tamarind as its base. It can feature various meats, such as pork, shrimp, beef, or fish, along with an assortment of vegetables like radish, eggplant, and string beans.
Kare-kare
P69
Kare-Kare is a rich and creamy Filipino stew made with oxtail, tripe, and/or pork hock, cooked in a peanut-based sauce. It is typically served with a variety of blanched vegetables like string beans, eggplant, and banana blossoms.
Bicol Express
P69
Bicol Express is a spicy Filipino dish made with pork, shrimp, or fish, cooked in coconut milk with chili peppers and shrimp paste.

Drinks

RC Cola
P35
RC Cola is a popular soft drink known for its smooth and crisp cola taste. Established globally, RC has made its mark in the Philippines, captivating consumers with its balanced sweetness and refreshing carbonation. With a rich cola flavor, it competes with other well-known cola brands and has earned a loyal following for its affordability and enjoyable taste.
Sprite
P35
Sprite is a zesty and lemon-lime flavored carbonated soft drink that exudes a bright and citrusy profile. Loved for its distinct non-cola taste, Sprite delivers a burst of effervescence that instantly refreshes and quenches thirst. Its clear and sparkling appearance, combined with its clean, tangy flavor, has made it a timeless choice for those seeking a crisp and uplifting beverage.
Royal Tru-Orange
P35
Royal Tru-Orange is a beloved Filipino soft drink cherished for its delightful orange essence. Produced by Coca-Cola Philippines, this vibrant soda offers a burst of natural orange flavor that captivates the taste buds with its sweetness and tanginess. With a refreshing and sunny disposition, Royal Tru-Orange has become a staple beverage choice in the Philippines, synonymous with quenching one's thirst on warm, tropical days.
